## What changed

- Guest user configuration supports more flexible storefront access.
- Self-service quotes let buyers convert quotes into orders directly.
- Search precision improves through larger category indexing and facet limits.
- Search relevance can be tuned by applying weights to product fields.
- Revenue Cloud recipes and store-specific procedure plans help manage more complex pricing.
