AgentExchange is Salesforce's marketplace that brings together AppExchange, AgentExchange, and Slack Marketplace.

## What changed

- Security controls in Connected Apps and ECAs should be updated before May 11, 2026.
- The updated AgentExchange homepage and navigation are automatically available when you visit the new AgentExchange experience.
- Admins can switch between Salesforce and Slack-specific views with the new navigation switcher.
- AgentExchange recommendations are embedded in Agentforce Builder so partners can be discovered in context.
- The update combines a security deadline with a new marketplace navigation model and in-context partner discovery.

## Notes for Agents

- This section is mostly about ecosystem and marketplace alignment.
- Treat the security-control deadline as the operational item and the new marketplace experience as the UX change.
- Use the linked articles when you need the security or marketplace-migration details separately.
