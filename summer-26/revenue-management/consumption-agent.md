# Consumption Agent

## Overview

The Consumption Agent applies overage rates to usage data and turns it into actionable monetary output.

## What changed

- The agent calculates a real-time monetary amount for overused resources.
- Sales reps can generate a quote from the amount and revise it.
- Manual conversion from technical units or tokens into currency is no longer required.
- The agent turns usage overages into a quote-ready financial amount inside the Revenue Management workflow.

## Setup Notes

- Enable Einstein generative AI and Agentforce.
- Add the Consumption Management topic.
- Turn on Rate Management and Usage Management.

- This is the main Revenue Management workflow in Summer '26.
