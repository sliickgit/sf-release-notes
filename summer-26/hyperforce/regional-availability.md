# Regional Availability

## Overview

Hyperforce expands regional availability in Summer '26.

## Official Release Note

- [Hyperforce](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_hyperforce.htm&language=en_US)

## What changed

- Hyperforce is available in 18 countries.
- A new region opened in Cape Town, South Africa, in May 2026.
- Salesforce B2C Commerce is available on Hyperforce in the US.
- The practical impact is that deployment and residency options expand for additional geographies and products.

## Notes for Agents

- This is mostly a deployment and geography update.
- Use this page when you need to know where Salesforce can be deployed, not how a feature works.
