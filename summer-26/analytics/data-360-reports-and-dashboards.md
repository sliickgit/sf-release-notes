# Data 360 Reports and Dashboards

## Overview

Data 360 reporting gets broader data support and better drill-down behavior.

## What changed

- Currency fields can now appear in Data 360 reports and dashboards, which makes financial reporting easier to express without workarounds.
- Calculated Insight Objects can surface history so reports can show trends over time instead of only current-state values.
- Dimensional hierarchies improve drill-down behavior for aggregated reporting data, so analysts can move from a summary view to a more specific breakdown more efficiently.
