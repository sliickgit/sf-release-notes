# Field Service Scheduling and Optimization

## Overview

Scheduling and optimization remain core themes in Field Service.

This page captures the operational scheduling improvements that support dispatchers and mobile workers.

## What changed

- Scheduling and optimization are highlighted as major themes in Summer '26.
- The release notes call out the new scheduling console.
- Routing quality improves through the enhanced Same Site Service Objective.
- Nearby appointments can be grouped to improve mobile worker efficiency.
- Home-base travel can be excluded to accelerate service arrival times.
- Travel time estimates for trucks can be customized for better schedule accuracy.
