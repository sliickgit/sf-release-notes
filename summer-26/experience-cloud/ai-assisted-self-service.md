## What changed

- Agentforce Orchestrator now works with the prompt bar, suggestion themes, dynamic content tiles, the Concierge sidebar, and chat history as one self-service stack.
- The configuration is meant to deliver personalized, conversational self-service experiences for both authenticated and guest users.
- The feature applies to Aura and LWR sites with an active community license, so this is the Experience Cloud surface to check when sites need AI-assisted service behavior.

## Notes for Agents

- This is the core Agentforce-enabled Experience Cloud change in Summer '26.
- If you are parsing what changed, the important distinction is that Salesforce is bundling several site components into one guided self-service experience rather than adding a single isolated widget.
