# Advanced Cross-Region Continuity

## Overview

Advanced Cross-Region Continuity improves recovery objectives for eligible Hyperforce orgs.

## What changed

- Recovery Time Objective is 12 hours.
- Recovery Point Objective is 4 hours.
- Data is replicated to a secondary geographic region.
- The feature is available as a Hyperforce premium add-on.

## Notes for Agents

- This is the core resiliency update in Hyperforce.
