## Overview

Customer Signals Intelligence surfaces real-time sentiment and contact insights.

## What changed

- Real-time sentiment insights are available for case, voice, and chat.
- Granular contact data can be grouped into meaningful collections.
- The goal is to identify customer pain points faster.
- This gives service teams a way to triage angry or at-risk customers sooner and adjust routing or follow-up accordingly.
- The feature is decision support for service leaders and supervisors, not a case-processing workflow by itself.

## Notes for Agents

- This is primarily a decision-support area for service leaders and operations teams.
- Treat this as an analytics and prioritization feature, not as a core case-processing change.
- Use the related help when you need the specific real-time insights or clustering behavior behind the summary.
