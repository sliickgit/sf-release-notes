# Agentforce Contact Center

## Overview

Agentforce Contact Center is Salesforce's AI-first contact center offering within Service.

The release notes position this as the primary contact-center modernization story for Summer '26.

## What changed

- The product is positioned as an AI-first contact center inside Salesforce.
- Voice, email, and messaging all appear as core contact-center areas in the release notes.
- The product is intended to redefine customer engagement with AI-led service workflows.
- Service teams can use Agentforce features to assist reps, handle conversations, and streamline customer engagement.
