# Co-Branded Emails for Partners

## Overview

Partners can send brand-compliant marketing content from Experience Cloud sites.

This is the distributed-marketing story for Partner Cloud.

## What changed

- Partners can personalize and send marketing content at scale without leaving the portal.
- The experience is designed to keep brand compliance intact.
- Partners can use the Experience Cloud site as the place to generate and send compliant content.
