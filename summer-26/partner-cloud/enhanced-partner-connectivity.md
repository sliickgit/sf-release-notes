# Enhanced Partner Connectivity

## Overview

Partner Cloud expands partner connectivity and secure record sharing.

## Official Release Note

- [Partner Cloud](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_partner_cloud.htm&language=en_US)

## What changed

- MuleSoft-powered flows can connect with many partners simultaneously.
- Account, opportunity, lead, contact, and case records can be shared.
- Record updates from your team and partners can be monitored.
- The change strengthens partner data synchronization and gives teams more visibility into shared records.
