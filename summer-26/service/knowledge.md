# Knowledge

## Overview

Knowledge includes a retirement notice and a migration target.

This section is mainly about planning the move away from Unified Knowledge.

## What changed

- Unified Knowledge is scheduled for retirement in Summer '27.
- Salesforce Enterprise Knowledge is the migration target.
- Enterprise Knowledge article URLs can be inserted into case emails in the broader Service area.
- The operational task here is migration planning, not feature adoption.
- The page matters because it tells teams when to start planning the knowledge migration path.
- The note is a transition map: one article for the retirement signal and one for the email-linking behavior.

## Notes for Agents

- Treat this as a migration-planning item rather than a feature update.
- If your service team uses Unified Knowledge today, treat this as a transition roadmap item.
- Use this page when you need to understand the path away from Unified Knowledge and into Enterprise Knowledge.
- If the question is about supported article URLs or deprecation timing, this is the right summary to read first.
- Follow the related help articles when you need the exact retirement or email-link implementation details.
