## What changed

- Agentforce for Field Service now includes builder support for scheduling agents.
- Prospects can book appointments without needing an existing account.
- Workers can schedule and reschedule appointments with Agentforce Employee Agent.
- Pre-work briefs can be configured with more dynamic data and interactive follow-up questions.
- The release notes frame this as a broader field-service agent toolkit rather than a single automation feature.
