# Financial Services

## Overview

Financial Services Cloud continues to use AI to unify banking, wealth, and insurance data.

## Official Release Note

- [Financial Services](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_fsc.htm)

## What changed

- Customer data from banking, wealth, and insurance platforms is unified.
- Personalized engagement is supported across the customer lifecycle.
- Faster onboarding is enabled through industry-specific automation.
- AI-powered services provide trusted insights for financial planning.
- The main theme is better consolidation of financial data with more AI support for planning and onboarding.
