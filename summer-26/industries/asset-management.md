# Asset Management

## Overview

Use Salesforce Go to manage, configure, and expand Asset Service Lifecycle Management features from a single location in Setup.

## Official Release Note

- [Asset Management](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_asset_management.htm)

## What changed

- Discover and configure Asset Service Lifecycle Management features in one place.
- Track feature usage from Salesforce Go.
- Access supporting content resources and configuration links.
- Purchase add-on licenses for some features directly from Salesforce Go when the Your Account app is enabled.

## Notes for Agents

- This is primarily a setup and feature-discovery improvement.
- It matters most for teams rolling out Asset Service Lifecycle Management at scale.
