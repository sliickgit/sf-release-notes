# HR Service

## Overview

HR Service gets more streamlined templates, privacy, and employee experience support.

The release intro also calls out migration and portal changes that affect employee-service operations.

The practical theme is easier setup for HR teams and a cleaner path for employee-service onboarding.

## What changed

- Prebuilt service templates simplify setup.
- Confidential case reporting improves privacy.
- The unified portal experience supports a more consistent employee experience.
- Unified licenses help standardize access and operations.
- Employees can be migrated from Customer Community Plus to the Unified Employee License.
- HR service setup can use prebuilt service templates to get started faster.
- HR Service now also centers on a prebuilt agent library for common HR tasks and a guided setup path through Salesforce Go.

## Notes for Agents

- This section is useful when HR case management is part of a shared service model.
- The main story is both privacy and faster onboarding of employee-service workflows.
- If the task is about employee support setup or migration, this is the page to treat as the HR Service summary.
- Use the linked HR Service articles when you need the migration, portal, or template-specific implementation details.
