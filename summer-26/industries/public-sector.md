# Agentforce Public Sector

## Overview

Agentforce Public Sector adds support for compliance, licensing, permitting, recruitment, and payments.

## Official Release Note

- [Agentforce Public Sector](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_public_sector_solutions.htm)

## What changed

- Agents can be built and deployed for Compliance Management.
- Licensing and Permitting workflows are supported.
- Talent Recruitment Management is supported.
- Inbound Payments provide a smoother constituent checkout experience.
- Outbound Payments help track payments made from external finance systems.
- The practical goal is to support more of the public-sector lifecycle with one Agentforce-backed workflow layer.

## Notes for Agents

- This section is a good fit for government and public-service automation scenarios.
- Use it when a public-sector workflow needs both compliance handling and transactional payment support.
