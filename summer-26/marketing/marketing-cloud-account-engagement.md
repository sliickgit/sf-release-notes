# Marketing Cloud Account Engagement

## Overview

Account Engagement gets email, consent, and opportunity-activity improvements.

## Official Release Note

- [Marketing](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_marketing_parent.htm&language=en_US)

## What changed

- Email management is improved.
- Consent syncing is simplified.
- Engagement activity can be viewed directly on Opportunity records.
- The practical effect is tighter visibility between marketing engagement, consent, and sales activity.

- This subsection is primarily about tracking engagement and consent more easily.
