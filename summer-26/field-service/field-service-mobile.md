# Field Service Mobile

## Overview

Field Service mobile workflows get better support for crew management and refresh behavior.

The mobile app is where the operational details of these updates are most visible.

## What changed

- Crew leads can manage crew timesheets in the mobile app.
- Users can refresh timesheet data with a pull-down gesture.
- The mobile app is the main runtime surface for timesheets and crew workflows in this section.

## Notes for Agents

- The mobile app is the main runtime surface for these changes.
- Use this page when you need the device-side workflow story rather than the broader scheduling or agent story.
