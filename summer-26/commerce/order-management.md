## What changed

- Routing logic can be modified and customized through agents and UI-based rule groups.
- The Order Management agent can offer tailored suggestions for your org.
- Delivery estimations are more accurate for more users.
- The feature family now splits into an agent-assisted routing story, proactive alerts, and more flexible delivery-estimation controls.
