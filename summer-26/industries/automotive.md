# Automotive

## Overview

Automotive gets conversational AI, automated workflows, guided service processes, and deeper analytics support.

## Official Release Note

- [Automotive](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_automotive_cloud.htm)

## What changed

- Conversational AI and automated workflows help streamline product sales and warranty claims.
- Prebuilt templates and screen flows simplify goodwill repairs and finance service processes.
- Salesforce Go bundles and unified consoles centralize telemetry, fleet data, connected vehicles, and remote actions.
- Tableau-powered analytics and Data Cloud provide a 360-degree view of dealer performance, vehicle portfolios, and lead intelligence.
- Autonomous agents and AI-assisted KYC validation support loan and lease servicing.
- CSV upload and mapping templates help load large volumes of vehicle, asset, and product data.
- Cross-cloud capabilities expand purchase order management, inventory tracking, and sample fulfillment.
- Agentforce for Automotive adds conversational summaries and dealer-portal support.
- Automotive Finance streamlines lease extensions and transaction disputes.

## Notes for Agents

- This is one of the most implementation-heavy Industries clouds in the release notes.
- The recurring theme is guided workflows plus AI-assisted dealer and service operations.
