# Net Zero

## Overview

Net Zero focuses on carbon accounting, energy estimation, and compliance discovery.

## Official Release Note

- [Net Zero](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_netzero.htm)

## What changed

- Automated data filling rules can estimate energy use for stationary assets.
- Market mechanisms can be applied directly to total CO2e values.
- Disclosure and Compliance Hub can be discovered and configured from Setup with Salesforce Go.
- The changes make sustainability data easier to configure, estimate, and report on without as much manual setup.

## Notes for Agents

- This area is mainly about operationalizing sustainability workflows.
- Use this page when the task involves carbon accounting, energy estimation, or compliance discovery setup.
