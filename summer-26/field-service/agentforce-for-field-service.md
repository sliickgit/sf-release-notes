# Agentforce for Field Service

## Overview

Agentforce features are being added directly into the Field Service experience.

This is the conversational automation slice of Field Service.

## What changed

- Agentforce is used to edit and delete timesheets through natural language.
- Agentforce is being used for workflow assistance in the mobile app.
- The release notes frame this as part of the larger shift toward agent-assisted field work.

## Notes for Agents

- This section is mainly about conversational task handling for field workers.
- Use it when you need to explain what field workers can ask Agentforce to do for them.
