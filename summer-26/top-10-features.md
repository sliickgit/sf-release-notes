# Summer '26 Top 10 Features

This is a subjective shortlist of the Summer '26 features with the highest business value, the strongest revenue impact, and the biggest security or risk-reduction value.

## Highest Business Value

1. [Apex default user-mode and sharing changes](development/apex.md) - broad platform leverage; it changes the security model for custom code.
2. [Agentforce Contact Center](service/agentforce-contact-center.md) - modernizes the core service channel experience.
3. [AI-Assisted Self-Service](experience-cloud/ai-assisted-self-service.md) - can reduce case volume and improve containment.
4. [Field Service Scheduling and Optimization](field-service/field-service-scheduling-and-optimization.md) - improves dispatch efficiency and technician utilization.
5. [Data 360 Reports and Dashboards](analytics/data-360-reports-and-dashboards.md) - improves decision-making over the customer/data layer.
6. [Consumption Agent](revenue-management/consumption-agent.md) - reduces manual usage-to-currency conversion work.
7. [Salesforce Mobile App updates](mobile/salesforce-mobile-app.md) - broad daily productivity gains for sellers and service users.
8. [Security Enhancements](security-identity-privacy/security-enhancements.md) - reduces security and compliance risk across the org.
9. [Salesforce Order Management](commerce/order-management.md) - streamlines order routing and delivery-estimation work.
10. [Agentforce Voice SIP Routing](agentforce/agentforce-voice-sip-routing.md) - gives voice teams a more flexible telephony path.

## Highest Revenue Impact

1. [Consumption Agent](revenue-management/consumption-agent.md) - turns usage into quote-ready financial output.
2. [Agentforce Contact Center](service/agentforce-contact-center.md) - improves service throughput and customer engagement.
3. [AI-Assisted Self-Service](experience-cloud/ai-assisted-self-service.md) - can deflect cases and improve digital support containment.
4. [Salesforce Order Management](commerce/order-management.md) - helps reduce fulfillment friction and routing delays.
5. [Marketing Cloud Next](marketing/marketing-cloud-next.md) - improves campaign execution and agent-assisted marketing workflows.
6. [Salesforce Mobile App updates](mobile/salesforce-mobile-app.md) - helps revenue teams act faster in the field.
7. [B2B Commerce](commerce/b2b-commerce.md) - improves storefront, pricing, and search behavior for buyers.
8. [B2C Commerce](commerce/b2c-commerce.md) - keeps monthly storefront and rollout changes easy to track.
9. [Field Service Scheduling and Optimization](field-service/field-service-scheduling-and-optimization.md) - improves dispatch and on-site execution efficiency.
10. [Partner Cloud](partner-cloud/enhanced-partner-connectivity.md) - supports partner data sharing and co-branded partner workflows.

## Highest Security and Risk Reduction

1. [Apex default user-mode and sharing changes](development/apex.md) - the biggest secure-by-default runtime shift in the release.
2. [Security Enhancements](security-identity-privacy/security-enhancements.md) - email verification, DKIM, and MFA hardening.
3. [Identity and Access Management](security-identity-privacy/identity-and-access-management.md) - retires weaker auth patterns and improves auditability.
4. [Salesforce Connect Cross-Org Adapter](customization/salesforce-connect-cross-org-adapter.md) - moves cross-org auth toward named credentials.
5. [Health Check](security-identity-privacy/health-check.md) - makes org-health monitoring more proactive.
6. [Security Center](security-identity-privacy/security-center.md) - improves posture visibility with better alert metrics.
7. [Salesforce Shield](security-identity-privacy/salesforce-shield.md) - strengthens scanning and transaction-security support.
8. [Experience Cloud Security and Sharing](experience-cloud/security-and-sharing.md) - tightens trust gating on sites and files.
9. [Named Credentials](security-identity-privacy/named-credentials.md) - makes external auth more consistent and easier to reason about.
10. [Release Updates](release-updates.md) - catches behavior-changing enforcement items that can break compatibility if ignored.
