# AI-Assisted Self-Service

## Overview

Experience Cloud now includes a suite of AI-assisted Self-Service components.

## What changed

- Agentforce Orchestrator, the prompt bar, suggestion themes, dynamic content tiles, the Concierge sidebar, and chat history can be configured together.
- The components support personalized, conversational self-service for authenticated and guest users.
- The feature applies to Aura and LWR sites with an active community license.

## Notes for Agents

- This is the core Agentforce-enabled Experience Cloud change in Summer '26.
