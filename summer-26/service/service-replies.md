# Service Replies

## Overview

Service Replies gains workflow refinements and AI-assisted drafting support.

This is the message-level service note where reps get faster reply handling, translation support, and assistant-driven response help.

## What changed

- The Hide Post Button changes how agents control visible reply actions in existing workflows.
- Agentforce Service Assistant can provide instant responses for service reps.
- Service Replies can now be translated into additional languages, which helps teams work across locales.
- These updates make reply handling more controlled and more automation-friendly without changing the surrounding case workflow.

- This is the most message-centric part of the Service section.
