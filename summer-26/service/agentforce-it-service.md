## What changed

- Uses AI-driven automation to reduce manual ticketing.
- Helps teams shift to proactive service workflows.
- Improves employee productivity through Slack and Microsoft Teams.
- Supports IT compliance management with centralized governance.
- Gives visibility into hardware lifecycle management from procurement to disposal.
- IT Service now spans setup automation, compliance workflows, hardware lifecycle management, self-service, collaboration channels, and bidirectional CMDB synchronization.
- The release notes frame it as a unified service-operations platform rather than a single ITSM feature.
