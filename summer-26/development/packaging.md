# Packaging

## Overview

Packaging continues to support app distribution and controlled upgrade timing.

## What changed

- You can now apply an expiration period to customized push upgrades.
- Partners can let subscribers block push upgrades for a limited time window before the upgrade expires.
- The change applies to both first-generation and second-generation managed packages.

## Notes for Agents

- This is most relevant when you need to control subscriber upgrade timing in regulated environments.
