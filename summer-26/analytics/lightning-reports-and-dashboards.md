# Lightning Reports and Dashboards

## Overview

Lightning reports and dashboards get visual consistency and extensibility improvements.

## Official Release Note

- [Reports and Dashboards](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_rd_reports_dashboards.htm&language=en_US)

## What changed

- Brand color palettes let you apply standardized colors across reports and dashboards.
- Custom Lightning Web Components can be embedded directly into dashboards for richer interactive visuals.
- The reporting experience gets more brand-consistent and more extensible at the dashboard layer.

## Notes for Agents

- This is the main reporting-UX improvement area in the analytics section.
- Use this page when the change is about how dashboards look or how they host custom interactive content.
- The useful signal here is that reports and dashboards are getting both branding and extensibility improvements.
