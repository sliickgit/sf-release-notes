## Overview

Entitlements and Milestones adds automated SLA tracking and proactive risk awareness.

## What changed

- SLA progress can be automated, predicted, and communicated.
- Agentforce can complete routine milestones like first responses.
- Proactive risk scoring helps identify cases nearing SLA breach.
- The change is about turning SLA tracking into an active workflow instead of a passive timer.
- The page breaks the SLA story into automated milestone handling and predictive risk scoring.
