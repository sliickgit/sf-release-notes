## Representative Cloud Updates

- Asset Management: manage, configure, and expand Asset Service Lifecycle Management from Setup with Salesforce Go.
- Automotive: use conversational AI, automated workflows, guided warranty and finance processes, unified consoles, and Tableau-powered analytics.
- Consumer Goods: collaborate with agents during customer visits, switch between planning modes, and deploy Tableau Next dashboards.
- Energy & Utilities: personalize energy plan comparisons, manage grid connection requests, and improve timesheet accuracy for field technicians.
- Financial Services: unify banking, wealth, and insurance data for personalized engagement and AI-powered services.
- Health: accelerate setup, generate assessment suggestions, and manage outreach, insurance comparisons, and pricing transparency.
- Manufacturing: extend Salesforce Go to include Tableau Next Analytics and the Distributor Management System.
- Media: manage multi-channel ad placements, AI-generated proposals, campaign targeting, and B2B/B2C promotions.
- Net Zero: automate data filling rules, align carbon accounting with GHG protocols, and discover compliance tools from Setup.
- Nonprofits: support fundraising, programs, grantmaking, outcomes, and volunteers with Agentforce Nonprofit.
- Agentforce Public Sector: support compliance, licensing, permitting, talent recruitment, and payments.
