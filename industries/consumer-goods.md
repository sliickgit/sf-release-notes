# Consumer Goods

## Overview

Consumer Goods focuses on mobile collaboration, planning, analytics, and sync troubleshooting.

## What changed

- Agents can collaborate during a customer visit in the Consumer Goods Cloud mobile app.
- Teams can switch between dynamic and fixed product planning.
- BOM components can be aggregated on standard products within business plans.
- Retail Execution Tableau Next dashboards can be deployed by using Salesforce Go.
- Default sync settings and trace mode KPIs help troubleshoot sync issues faster.

## Notes for Agents

- This section combines field collaboration with planning and analytics.
- The Tableau Next dashboards are a notable setup and rollout detail.
