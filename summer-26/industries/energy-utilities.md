# Energy & Utilities

## Overview

Energy & Utilities gets personalized self-service, sync notifications, grid request tracking, and stronger field-service timesheet handling.

## Official Release Note

- [Energy & Utilities](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=release-notes.rn_energy_and_utilities_cloud.htm)

## What changed

- Guest users and existing customers can compare energy plans and self-enroll.
- Slack notifications are sent when data sync operations finish for multisite quotes and auto quotes.
- Grid connection requests can be managed with milestone-based tracking and SLA monitoring.
- Field technicians get improved timesheet accuracy through enhanced summary views, automatic idle time entries, editable rejected timesheets, and mobile refresh gestures.

## Notes for Agents

- This cloud blends customer self-service with operational field work.
- The timesheet and grid-request workflows are the main concrete changes in the extracted notes.
