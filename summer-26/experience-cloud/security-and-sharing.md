# Security and Sharing

## Overview

Experience Cloud adds email verification, password-flow restrictions, and malware protection.

## What changed

- Domain-level and user-level verification is required to send email from a site.
- People using public email services can send email from the site after the new requirements are enabled.
- Private LWR pages stay unavailable until password setup or reset is complete.
- Malware scanning for Salesforce Files is generally available.
- The Manage Malicious Files permission can be assigned to standard users.

## Notes for Agents

- These are the security and trust controls most likely to matter in a site rollout.
